About Us

Presbyterian Church in Berlin
Founded in 2015, the Presbyterian Church in Berlin serves as a vibrant community for worshippers seeking spiritual growth and fellowship. Over the years, it has evolved into a center of faith and outreach, embracing diversity and fostering unity among its members.

General History of the Presbyterian Church
The Presbyterian Church in Cameroon (PCC) is a prominent Reformed and Presbyterian denomination in the country. Established by the Basel Mission in 1886, it grew to become the largest English-speaking church in Cameroon, contributing significantly to religious, health, and educational services nationwide.

Originating from the Basel Mission's endeavors, the PCC expanded its reach across Southern Cameroon, establishing mission stations and translating scriptures into local languages by the late 19th century. It gained autonomy in 1957, continuing its mission to spread the Christian faith and provide essential services to communities across Cameroon.
